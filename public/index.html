How it works

Each quiz has 20 questions plus a few surprise bonus questions worth double points. Multiple-choice, true/false, and short-answer questions are all in the mix. Wrong-answer options are generated by an AI on the fly — closer to the right answer on harder difficulties.

Your score appears at the end. Leaderboards are kept for each level and an all-time board across all three. Quick Mode players who score 90% or more get their own leaderboards too.
